GLOSSYQ - YOUR PRIVACY CHOICES

Last Updated: January 2026

A2Bsolutions Ltd respects your privacy. Here is how you can manage your data:

DELETE YOUR ACCOUNT

Permanently delete all your personal data from our systems.

Open the GlossyQ app → Go to Profile → Scroll to bottom → Tap "Close Account"

EXPORT YOUR DATA

Receive a file containing all your personal information.

Open the GlossyQ app → Go to Profile → Tap "Export My Data"

OPT OUT OF COMMUNICATIONS

We only send essential service-related communications.

We do not send marketing emails.

MANAGE SUBSCRIPTIONS

Manage or cancel your subscription through Apple.

Go to iPhone Settings → Tap your Apple ID → Subscriptions → GlossyQ

UPDATE YOUR INFORMATION

You can update your profile information at any time.

Open the GlossyQ app → Go to Profile settings

DATA REQUESTS

For any privacy-related requests or questions, contact us. We respond to all privacy requests within 48 hours.
